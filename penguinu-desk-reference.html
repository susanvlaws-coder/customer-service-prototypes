Secure Exam Break Protocol

Quick Reference Guide & Waiver Matrix

1. Initial Verification (ADA Check)

Before approving any break request, you must check the student's profile for an active ADA Accommodation Letter.

  • If YES (Has Letter): STOP. Do not use this protocol. Follow the specific instructions outlined in their individual accommodation letter.
  • If NO (Standard Student): Proceed to Step 2.

2. Expectation Setting (The Golden Rules)

To protect exam integrity and prevent escalated complaints, you must read the following script verbatim to the student before authorizing the break feature.

Read Aloud to Student: "You may take a 10-minute Secure Exam Break. However:

1. You CANNOT return to any question you have already viewed.
2. You CANNOT save unused minutes for later.
3. You MUST complete a room check with the proctor upon return."

Action: If the student declines these terms, instruct them to continue their exam without utilizing the break feature.

3. Execution & Proctor Handoff

Once the student accepts the terms:

  • Instruct the student to click the "Secure Exam Break" button on their dashboard.
  • Remind the student they must engage the chat with their Proctor before leaving the camera view.
  • Document the interaction in the CRM.

4. Fee Waiver Decision Matrix ($60 Value)

If an issue occurs during the break process and the student requests a complimentary retake, use the matrix below to determine the correct financial action.

✅ APPROVE WAIVER Medical or Tech Emergency

Fire alarms, verified internet outages, or clear medical emergencies.

✅ APPROVE WAIVER Staff Error (Service Recovery)

Call logs indicate the agent failed to read the verbatim script regarding policy rules.

❌ DENY WAIVER Student Error / Ignorance

Student claims "I didn't know the rule" or panicked, despite logs showing the script was read.

❌ DENY WAIVER Time Banking Violations

Student attempted to split their 10 minutes into multiple smaller breaks.
